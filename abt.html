Welcome to Our Mission

At Haritha Karmasena, we are dedicated to transforming communities by promoting clean streets, better hygiene practices, and preventing diseases. Together, we can create a healthier, sustainable future for everyone. The Haritha Karmasena campaign is committed to fostering a cleaner and healthier environment for all. By engaging communities in regular street clean-up drives, we aim to instill a sense of pride in our surroundings.Our hygiene initiatives focus on educating the public about sanitation practices, providing essential facilities, and promoting health awareness.
